The park

More than 500 m2 of totally enclosed and safe space to play, learn, wake up, build... To let off steam without limits, a unique place dedicated to children. Our park is heated in winter and air-conditioned in summer, free parking is available for your convenience. Everything is done for the well-being of the families who visit us!

In a unique, colourful and lively setting, you will find a relaxation area for parents (magazines, wifi, etc.) and games for children from 0 to 12 years old.

Several games are available:

- Slides
- Trampolines
- Tyrolean
- Monkey Bridge
- Giant Legos
- Ball pool
- motor skills course
- ...

A space just for toddlers (0-2 years) Parents or accompanying persons are welcome to play with their children.

Luzi Parc is a fun place that allows you to spend entire days dedicated to the happiness and fun of children!

During your visit to Luzi Parc, a snack/bar is at your disposal to eat at any time, with a wide variety, for children and adults!

For pleasure and conviviality:

- Hot drinks: coffee, tea, hot chocolate, cappuccino...
- Cold drinks: soft drinks, orange juice, water...

For those with a sweet tooth:

- Waffles: sugar, Nutella, cream...
- Crepes: Nutella, sugar....
- Croque Monsieur, Paninis
- Ice cream...

Everything you need to have a good time with your family or friends!!!

NB:

- Children are under the supervision and responsibility of parents or accompanying adults.
- The use of socks is mandatory.
- Personal snacks (except water) are not accepted.
